**Date:** January 2026
**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/retentionadam_the-only-metrics-that-matter-at-each-stage-activity-7416134798374510592-JIP7?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop&rcm=ACoAAFJkeLEBrvXP2UgDDYzykAJzS1BbJ8VM1rI
**Content:** The only metrics that matter at each stage of SaaS:

$0-500K ARR: Weekly MRR growth rate
$1-5M ARR: Weekly MRR growth rate
$5-10M ARR: Churn + Top of funnel growth

Yes, it’s the same metric twice, but weekly MRR growth rate is the ONLY thing that matters from 0-$5M.

Are you growing week over week? If so, by how much?

Keep it simple. Track the number that tells you if you're moving forward or not.

Churn doesn't matter until $5M+. Before that, you don't have enough customers for the data to mean anything. 

Once you hit $5M, churn starts eating you alive if you ignore it. That's when it matters.

Top of funnel growth becomes the game at $5-10M. You've got the product, you've got retention. Now it's about feeding the machine.

But before $5M…

Weekly MRR growth rate ONLY.
